How HID Works

Four (4) steps that show exactly how HID connects you and your hospital without stress or paperwork.

01

Create Your HID ACCOUNT

Patient signs up, verifies identity and links to NIN/SSN. A unique health ID is generated and secured with OTP verification.

02

Link Your Health Records

Hospitals upload or sync your medical history. Lab results, prescriptions, and visit notes are securely attached to your HID profile.

03

Access Anywhere

Patient uses HID or NIN at any hospital. Your records are instantly accessible to verified medical staff across the country.

04

Hospital Pulls Your Data

Hospital retrieves your complete patient file instantly. No paperwork, no delays — verified records delivered securely in seconds.
